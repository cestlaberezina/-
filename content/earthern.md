This [[exhibit]]ion took place in March 2020 at Fotografia Gallery in [[tbilisi]], following several months of re[[search]] and [[art]]istic [[work]] from august 2019 to march 2020.

The project centered around [[earth]] — both as a [[medium]] and a [[concept]]ual framework, [[engag]]ing with [[loc]]al re[[source]]s, [[environment]]al concerns, and cross-[[disciplin]]ary [[cult]]ural [[integr]]ion. The works explored the [[transformation]] of [[raw]], [[site-specific]] elements into artistic [[express]]ion, bridging fine art, embodiment practices, and activism.

The exhibition included:
- [[sculpt]]ures made from [[wild]] [[clay]] sourced in Ajara and [[soil]] from the Rioni valley
- a [[film]] examining the [[eco]]logical and [[social]] [[impact]] of the Namakhvani dam
- a [[happenning]] incorporating soil from Namakhvani, [[merg]]ing [[movement]], [[matter]]riality, and [[protest]]. recorded and screened throughout the whole exhibition
- a clay-on-canvas piece as a result of the happening

displayed works: [[brili]], [[rv sculpture]], [[rv happening]], [[content/uplistsikhe]], [[idbfm]], [[imiom]], [[palpation]]. additionally, the exhibition presented a guest work: a documentary on namakhvani by marina kaganova and leo descristoforo.
