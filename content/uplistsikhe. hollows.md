This series follows an Armenian musician playing flute through the ancient [[hollow]]s of one of Georgia’s oldest [[urban]] settlements. The flute, an instrument of [[breath]] and [[resonan]]ce, converses with the [[cave]]s, [[amplif]]ying the [[sound]] of emptiness and [[presence]] alike. The work explores sound, presence, and the interplay between body and ancient architectural space. "Uplistsikhe isn’t a place — it’s a [[pause]] in geography, a kind of [[hesit]]ation carved into stone." Uplistsikhe, Georgia, 2023.
