This series of hand-crafted micro sculptures, made from white local chamotte clay, interprets the Taoist eight trigrams studied by the artist since 2017. Minimal forms, finished with traditional milking techniques and pierced for incense offerings, explore the space between material reality and structured philosophical symbolism. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2021. Fired at 1050°c, each 3-5 cm
