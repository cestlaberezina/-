This series documents a live shibari performance in Tbilisi on December 18, 2022, exploring intimacy, control, and physical presence through rope and movement. The work emphasizes trust and negotiation, using monochrome photography to capture the interplay of bodies, space, and gesture. With [[shibari]] artist Gary S4B and musician Nick Hudson. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2022.
