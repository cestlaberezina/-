This series follows a figure moving within the Shamkoretsots Sourb Astvatsatsin Church, an 18th-century Armenian structure damaged by the 1989 earthquake and threatened by urban development. By introducing foil into the space, the artist transmits catastrophe not through spectacle, but through reflection: fragments of light bounce off metal, echoing both the shimmer of divine iconography and the harsh glare of neglect. This series is rendering a space once shaped by patriarchal religious power into a site of living feminist ritual. Shamkoretsots Sourb Astvatsatsin Church, Tbilisi, Georgia, 2024.
