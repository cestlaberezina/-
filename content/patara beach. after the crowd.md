This series depicts a nude male figure moving across Patara Beach, Kaş, Turkey, emphasizing presence within an emptied landscape. The work reflects the beach’s seasonal abandonment and natural indifference. The landscape does not welcome, nor does it reject; it simply continues, and the body, brief and searching, learns to move within its indifference. Patara Beach, Kaş, 2024.
