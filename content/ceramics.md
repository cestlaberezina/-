This series of ceremonial vessels and bowls explores the intersection of materiality and spirituality, emphasizing voids that invite personal [[practic]]e. Following the closure of her urban studios in September 2021, the artist shifted to a practice centered on a wood-burning kiln in natural settings, embracing slower, elemental processes. Georgia and Russia, 2021–2025.
