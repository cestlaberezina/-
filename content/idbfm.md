This series consists of two vessels made from unprocessed Brili clay and self-made glazes, fired at 1050°C. The work emphasizes the material’s inherent unpredictability and the artist’s direct engagement, creating a dialogue between form, texture, and spontaneous surface effects. "I [[Decor]]ated the [[Body]] From the Inside": in this work, the artist explores the raw potential of [[wild]] [[brili]] [[clay]], shaping it solely from the inside. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2025.
