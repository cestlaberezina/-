"i [[decor]]ated the [[body]] from the inside": in this work, the artist explores the raw potential of [[wild]] [[brili]] [[clay]], shaping it solely from the inside. [[through]] an [[intuit]]ive [[process]] of manual application, combined with the blending of [[self-made]] [[glaz]]es, a [[gold]]en form [[emerg]]es on both vessels. this [[experiment]] highlights the un[[predict]]able [[beauty]] of the [[material]] and the artist’s hands-on [[engag]]ement with it. the result is a delicate yet powerful interplay between form, texture, and the spontaneous interaction of glazes.

two vessels, unprocessed brili clay, glazes, fired at 1050℃. exhibited at [[earthern]]
