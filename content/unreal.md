>-It is this not-I of mine that enchants the [[ego]] of the dreamer, and it is this that [[poetry]] know how to communicate to us. It is my not-I that allows my dreaming ego to [[experience]] its [[faith]] in [[being]] in the [[world]]. In the face of [[the real]] world, one can [[discovery]] in oneself the being of [[care]]. We are thrown into the world, [[abandon]]ed to the inhumanity of the world, to its negativity; the world is thus the non-being of man. The [[demand]]s ([[tyranny]]) of our functioning in [[reality]] oblige us to [[adapt]] to reality, to constitute ourselves as reality, to create works that are realities. But does not the dream [[essence]]tially free us from the functioning of reality?
