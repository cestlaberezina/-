This series shows a figure moving across the Tbilisi Reservoir, a mid-20th-century artificial site now marked by silt and ecological neglect. The work traces gestures of presence and resistance, reflecting human and environmental histories without narrative closure. "The woman is not separate from the land. She is another erosion." Tbilisi Reservoir, Tbilisi, 2023.
