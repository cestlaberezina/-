the book was never finished, as if water itself had [[interrupt]]ed it. _water etudes_ began beneath the [[surface]] at cap roig, where bodies become outlines and outlines [[dissol]]ve. photography tried to [[hold]] something, drawing tried to [[expla]]in it, and both [[fail]]ed in beautiful ways. the images remain [[draft]]s, like [[thought]]s never spoken a[[loud]]. the project unfolded not toward [[complet]]ion but toward [[evapora]]tion. what stays is a certain [[rhythm]], an [[immers]]ion, a rhythm again — of bodies in water, of [[time]] without [[punctu]]ation. perhaps what [[remain]]s is [[enough]]. perhaps all that is incomplete is already complete in being [[attempt]]ed.

is an un[[fin]]ished book project that still carries the clarity of [[intent]]ion and [[poet]] strength in its intermediate form. shot [[underwater]] at cap roig, it captures fleeting [[gesture]]s and bodily lines in conversation with the [[fluid]]ity and [[resist]]ance of [[water]]. the idea was to superimpose drawings of Labellecicatrice on Paulines photographs. though the book remains incomplete, the process itself holds a [[resonan]]ce, [[celebrat]]ing different approaches to art and [[complement]]arity.
