In this series, photographed in 2024 in the waters surrounding Cap Roig on Spain’s Mediterranean coast, the artist uses underwater photography to investigate contemporary masculinity within a coastal ecological context. The artist works with a nude male figure, positioning him among reefs and shallow rock formations, allowing the body to move according to the currents and natural light. The project forms part of a broader inquiry into ecological embodiment and the visual transformations that occur when human subjects interact directly with marine environments.
