This work, created from raw local clay, draws inspiration from Georgian Stone Age textures and remnants. Using unrefined material, the artist explores direct expression and a connection to the land, emphasizing simplicity, authenticity, and the dialogue between past and present. "I Made It Out of [[Mud]]". Two vessels, local clay, glazes, fired at 1050℃. Exhibited at [[earthern]].  Tbilisi, Georgia, 2021
