"i made it out of [[mud]]": inspired by georgian stone age remnants and textures, this work is crafted from [[raw]] local [[clay]] — in its least prepared state. the artist [[believ]]es that [[truth]] [[emerg]]es from the [[simpl]]est [[material]]s, allowing creation to [[unfold]] in its [[pure]]st form. through this unrefined [[medium]], the work channels an authentic [[connect]]ion to the [[land]], [[embrac]]ing the rawness of the material as a [[direct]] path to [[express]]ion. the result is a [[dialogue]] between past and present, simplicity and [[depth]], where the very [[nature]] of the clay becomes [[integr]]al to the truth.

two vessels, local clay, glazes, fired at 1050℃. exhibited at [[earthern]]
