This series follows a figure moving through meadows between Georgia and Armenia, exploring gestures of exile and displacement. The work reflects the experience of those who fled Russia after the outbreak of war in Ukraine. "They say borders are drawn on maps, but i’ve seen them bloom in the chest, like bruises you didn’t know you had. Somewhere between Georgia and Armenia, the [[land]] [[forget]]s to be divided." Udabno, Georgia, 2023.
