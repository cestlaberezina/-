This collection, created for the Georgian jewelry brand by Nina Ivanova and Giorgi Wazovski, consists of sculptural ceramic forms inspired by the textures of Georgia’s caves and eroded landscapes. Utilizing natural clay and pigments, the work embraces unpredictability, highlighting the traces of fire, pressure, and process in each piece. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2022
