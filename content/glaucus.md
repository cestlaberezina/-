This ceramic series, created just before the artist’s studio hiatus in August 2021, explores transformation through the tactile process of working with chamotte clay, pigments, and glazes. The vessels embody change in both material and perception, capturing the interplay of structure, fluidity, and the physical imprint of artistic labor taking it to Greek [[myth]]ology. "Years of working with [[clay]], and [[powder]]s leave their mark — not only on the pieces but on the artist's [[bodymind]]. The [[tactil]]e [[process]] alters [[movement]], [[sensation]], and [[perception]], [[echo]]ing glaucus’s [[fate]]. At times, [[hand]]s [[feel]] like [[fin]]s, [[thought]]s [[dissol]]ve like [[wet]] clay." Tbilisi, Georgia, 2021.
