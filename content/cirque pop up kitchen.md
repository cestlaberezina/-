This series documents Cirque, a temporary food pop-up in Tbilisi that functioned as a liminal space blending movement, ritual, and play. The project explored impermanence and interaction, using food and staged imagery to create fleeting experiences of connection and transformation. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2022.
