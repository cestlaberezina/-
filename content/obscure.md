[[tbilisi]], 24.12–30.12.2021  
works & curation: pauline pelagia berezina; sound: tete noise; opening sound: santebela; space: eleven window

in a world [[obsess]]ed with [[clarity]], [[meaning]], and pleasant re[[solution]]s, this exhibition [[challeng]]es the [[need]] for [[certain]]ty. we carry [[stigma]]s about [[emotion]]s — [[categoriz]]ing them as good, productive, or meaningful, while everything else becomes discomfort or [[suffer]]ing. [[celebrat]]ing, we usually [[wish]] something to each other instead of [[witness]]ing who and where we are now as its [[ambiguity]]. [[real]]ity isnt clear or clean, but [[divers]]e.

this project, unfolding through prints and a happening ([[emerge]]) recorded & screened, is offering a [[safe]] enough [[space]] to feel the [[mess]] of the life. reality is neither [[fix]]ed nor comprehensible, yet its [[weight]] is undeniable. feeling that weight—without [[rush]]ing to [[defin]]e or [[escap]]e it — is, in itself, a [[practic]]al [[mirac]]le.
