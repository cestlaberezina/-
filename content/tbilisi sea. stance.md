This series depicts a figure at the Tbilisi Sea, a mid-20th-century artificial reservoir facing ecological pressures from pollution and urban use. The work contrasts human presence with the altered landscape, reflecting the city’s environmental and infrastructural challenges. Tbilisi Sea, Tbilisi, 2023.
