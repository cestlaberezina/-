###### methodology  
i focus on nature and subjectivity in people and use motor imagination (movement) as a method to unfold it, encouraging clients to sense their body and interact with inner and outer landscape. big part of the process is safety -- holding space for intense processes (in trauma informed approach) and containing whatever happens there. i always say that my camera is not a gun but a little black box, rather listening than watching. ill guide you during the shoot, if necessary. i appreciate transparency and do not support any discrimination
