This series developed in response to the exhibition [[earthern]], where the artist spent 14 days interacting with viewers alongside her work. Using raw clay on paper, the pieces record gestures and exchanges between artist, material, and audience, capturing the imprint of time and presence. Tbilisi, Georgia, 2021.
