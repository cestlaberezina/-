This hand-sculpted concrete table channels the rugged landscapes of Georgia, its undulating surface echoing mountains and riverbeds. Rooted in raw materiality, it integrates seamlessly with Le Chic Radical’s jewelry and natural elements, creating a space where design, geology, and craftsmanship converge. The piece functions as both a table and a sculptural statement, inviting touch, tracing, and engagement with the textures of the earth. Tbilisi, 2022.
