This performance, filmed by Julia Balina in Saint Petersburg on January 5, 2020, transforms clay beyond its traditional ceramic form through bodily movement on a large canvas. The work explores the interaction of material, gesture, and environment, using the body as a medium to trace, imprint, and dissolve boundaries between self and matter. Saint Petersburg, Russia, 2020.
