This series presents still lifes of discarded materials collected along the Georgian Black Sea, showing the intersection of natural and synthetic elements. Waste, shells, and fruit remnants are arranged against the coastal landscape, reflecting human impact without judgment. Anaklia, Georgia, 2023.
