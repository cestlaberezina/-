The marriage with [Antoine Depreux](https://www.instagram.com/antoine_depreux/)  began as a [[politic]]al act of solidarity: to re[[claim]] [[human]] rights that had been taken from people. What started as a stance became an actual marriage, shaped by [[intent]]ion rather than [[convention]].

The celebration followed the same logic. Drawing on Roland [[Barthes]]’ “Comment vivre ensemble,” the [[space]] was designed as an [[experiment]] in co[[exist]]ence, provoking [[reflect]]ion on how humans in[[habit]] a shared world. The film “Planet Sauvage” was screened, and guests were partially [[blindfold]]ed to stimulate the [[question]]ing: [[commu]]nity as a common being or [[being-in-common]]?
